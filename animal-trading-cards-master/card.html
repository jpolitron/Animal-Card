Black Panther

Panthers were multi-colored in ancient mythology.
  • Scientific Name: Panthera Pardus
  • Average Length: 3-6.1 Ft
  • Average Lifespan: 12- 15 Years
  • Habitat: Hot, dense Tropical rainforests of south and southeast asia

Black coat fur or large black spots. The name black panther is applied to black-coated leopards which is where it gets the name Panthera paradus of Africa and Asia. They can also be called black leaopards and black jaguars. They reach up to 6.1 ft long.
